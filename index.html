About Kairos Beetec


Full line of beekeeping supplies for the beginning beekeper to advanced. Everything from hive products hive products, bee-keeping tools, bio-vectoring technologies, pollination services now, bee rearing, training (apiculture, meliponicolture, pollination ecology), agricultural extension services, pollinator conservation, consultancy services, and frames to extractors, bottling equipment and jars. We also have a line of protective clothing, honey labels and books on beekeeping, candlemaking and soapmaking.


Kairos Beetec is deeply immersed in the beekeeping community of Kemya and embedded in its history. We have been supplying and teaching Kenyan beekeepers since 2000. We manufacture a significant portion of our products to support the unique needs of the Kenyan diverse climate. We test out all of our products in our own apiaries. Beekeeping is unique among agricultural activities. It is based on a symbiotic relationship, in which we host the bees in the hope of pollination, surplus honey, and the pleasure of observing the sophisticated workings of this highly structured social organism called a beehive.
